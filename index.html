What are spells?

A spell is a controlled manifestation of magic that affects the world in a supernatural fashion. There are spells for conjuring objects out of thin air and vanishing objects into thin air, spells for transforming something into something else (transfiguration), spells for making object levitate or carry out certain actions (cahrms), as well as combative spells, like stunning and shielding.

The action of performing a spell is referred to as casting> or spellwork.>

Generally cast by a witch or a wizard, spells were often produced with certain wand movements and a verbal incantation, or for more experienced witches and wizards, silently and wandlessly.

Some spells could appear as jets of light. Because of this, spells could often miss, be dodged, or blocked, either with an object or another spell.

The Standard Book, Grade 1

Alohamora!

  • Type: Charm
  • Pronunciation: ah-LOH-ho-MOR-ah
  • Description:Unlocks doors and other locked objects. It can also unlock doors that have been sealed with a Locking Spell, although it is possible to bewitch doors to become unaffected by this spell.

Ascendio

Type: Charm

Pronunciation: ah-SEN-dee-oh

Description: Lifts the caster high into the air. The charm also works underwater, propelling the caster above the surface.
